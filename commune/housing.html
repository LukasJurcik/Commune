Housing

Apply for a home in Køge municipality, read about housing support and deposit loans.

There are five housing companies in Køge Municipality. It is primarily the individual housing company that provides housing. If you want a rental property, you must be registered with the housing company.

To be entered on a waiting list for a residence, you must be 15 years old. It is reasonable to be written up when you are 15 years old, even if you do not need the residence right now.

Køge Municipality provides housing in the following housing companies:
•Dansk Almennyttigt Boligselskab (DAB)

Køge Municipality has some homes that can only be designated after visitation and approval in the Social and Health Department. The properties are mainly 2-room housing (some 3-room homes) and are located in conjunction with other homes of the same category.

In order to be considered for a free choice of elderly housing, one must:

•Need a home with special facilities for the elderly and / or the disabled.
•Need a home that helps you maintain your skills and ensure that you can remain self-reliant to a large extent.
•As a starting point, receive a social pension.

Housing support is a subsidy for the rent. You can seek housing support if you live for rent. If you are a national or early retirement pension, you can also seek housing support for an owner-occupied or residential home.

Change address

Are you moving? You need to change your address and get a yellow card with new address.

Fastest way to change your address is online trough borger.dk or you can go to your local commune. Changing your address online:
1. Click here.
2. Choose city you are moving to
3. Click on Continue and Insert your NemID information
4. You will be redirected to a form where you have to fill up your personal information.
5. After that you will recieve new Yellow Card to your new address within a week.

Build & Renovate

Read about building permits, BBR and more.

Before you begin a new building, major renovation or other significant changes in a building, you must digitally apply for permission from us in the municipality. We process your building case and decide whether you can get a permit.

The BBR register contains information about properties, buildings, housing and commercial real estate. BBR is used in connection with statistics, payment of benefits, collection of taxes, assessment of real estate, etc.

Building and Housing Registration (BBR) The BBR register contains information about properties, buildings, housing and commercial real estate. BBR is used in connection with statistics, payment of benefits, collection of taxes, assessment of real estate, etc.

The BBR register shows the actual circumstances that exist on a property such as areas, materials, heating, toilet and bathing facilities. It is the individual municipality that changes and registers in BBR, but it is the owner who is responsible for the information in BBR.
