New in Denmark?

Here you can find all the neccessary steps to become legal citizen in Denmark

01

Residence Permit

If you are an EU citizen, in order to get a residence permit you need a valid ID or Passport with contract where is your address and if you are student also contract from school you are attending here in Denmark. After that you need to go to Statsforvaltningen and show them all documents mentoined above.

02

Yellow Card-CPR & NemID

After you recieve residence permit you can go to your local commune and ask for Yellow Card (Health Insurance Card) where you will have your CPR number. Don't forget your valid ID or Passport and ask for NemID. NemID is your Internet password that makes it safe for you to view and disclose confidential and sensitive information via the Internet.

03

Bank Account

In order to get a Bank account you need your Yellow Card, valid ID or Passport and also danish phone number. Having a Danish bank account will allow you to work legaly here in Demark. If you forgot to ask for NemID at your commune you can get it via your bank.
