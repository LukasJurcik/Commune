Self Service

Learn how to get NemID, change address and sign up for digital mail

NemID

NemID is your Internet password that makes it safe for you to view and disclose confidential and sensitive information via the Internet.

• You can get NemID either at your local commune (Don't forget your valid ID or Passport)
• You can ask for NemID at your Bank.

Change address

Are you moving? You need to change your address and get a yellow card with new address.

Fastest way to change your address is online trough borger.dk or you can go to your local commune. Changing your address online:
1. Click here.
2. Choose municipality you are moving to
3. Click on Continue and Insert your NemID information
4. You will be redirected to a form where you have to fill up your personal information.
5. After that you will recieve new Yellow Card to your new address within a week.

How to sign up for digital email

Digital Mail from Public Authorities is the same kind of letters that you already receive in your physical mailbox from, for example, the municipality, hospital or SKAT. Private companies, such as insurance companies and banks, also send digital mail to their customers.

• To read your digital mail at borger.dk, enter www.borger.dk/post or click on Digital Post at the top of the page. Then sign in with your NemID.
• To read your digital mail at e-Boks.dk, enter www.e-Boks.dk and click on "Sign in private". Then sign in with your NemID.
